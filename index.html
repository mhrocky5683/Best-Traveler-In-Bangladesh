Best Travel Vlogger In Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a country full of vibrant culture, natural beauty, and unique experiences. Travel vloggers from Bangladesh are becoming increasingly popular as they offer a glimpse into the country's diverse landscapes and rich cultural heritage. Bangladesh is a country that has a lot to offer to travelers who are looking to explore new cultures and experiences. Origin of name

The name of Sajek Valley came from the Sajek River that originates from Karnafuli river.
The Sajek river works as a border between Bangladesh and India

Location

Sajek is a union located in the north of Chittagong Hill Tracts.
It's under Baghaichori Upazila in Rangamati hill district, it is situated 67 kilometres (42 mi)
north-east from Khagrachhari town and 95 kilometres (59 mi) north from Rangamati city.
The border of Bangladesh and Mizoram of India is 8 kilometres (5.0 mi) east from Sajek

Nature

Sajek valley is known for its natural environment and is surrounded by mountains, dense forest, and grassland hill tracks.
Many small rivers flow through the mountains among which the Kachalong and the Machalong are notable.
